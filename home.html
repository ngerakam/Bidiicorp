Company's objective

Our objective is to ensure that the farmer has our full dedication towards ensuring that He/She is recieving the support of the company.
We want farmers all to have basic knowledge on dairy farming and up keep of the cattle.
We also want them to have a basic understanding on frequently occuring diseases and how to prevent them.
We also want them to have access to our resources like grants ,loans which we will implement by time.
