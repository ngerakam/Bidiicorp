about us

Bidii corp was formed by Leah Karimi (founder),Moses Ndungu (C.E.O) , Dalvine Odhiambo (Chairperson) and Dennis Kamau (C.I.O)
Our objective is to ensure that farmers increase their produce and pass on mordern farming equipments and training to farmers.

We mainly interact with farmers on a personal level as they can view good farming practices and advice on pests and diseases.
